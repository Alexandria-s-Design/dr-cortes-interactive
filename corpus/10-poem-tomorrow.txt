 Tomorrow

 
 

 by Carlos E. Cortés

 

 

 Tomorrow and tomorrow and tomorrow

 Recede relentlessly 

 From an assumption

 Into an 
expectation

 Followed by

 Hope

 Concern

 Fear

 And then resignation

 

 But finally, 

 if all goes well,

 a return to the ordinary pleasures

 Of reminiscing about having led

 A pretty good life

 And finally the unexpected jubilation

 Of the sun coming up

 One last time

 With my loved ones

 Glorious face on the pillow

 Next to me

 Still smiling
