 Carlos E. Cortes Award 
 
 Posted by carlosecortes on January 29, 2016 

 Posted in: Uncategorized . 
 
 2 Comments 

 On January 28, 2016, the City of Riverside, California, announced the naming of an annual award in my name to be given to a Riverside resident who has made outstanding contributions to diversity and inclusion in the community.  The award is entitled the Carlos E. Cortes Award.
