 Degrees and Experience

                Degrees

 B.A., Communications and Public Policy, University of California, Berkeley, 1956

 M.S., Journalism, Columbia University, 1957

 Bachelor of Foreign Trade, American Institute for Foreign Trade (now Thunderbird Graduate School of Global Management), 1962

 M.A., Portuguese and Spanish, University of New Mexico, 1965

           Experience

 Lecturer throughout the United States, Latin America, Europe, Asia, and Australia

 Featured presenter on the Video Journal of Education’s training video, “Diversity in the Classroom”
